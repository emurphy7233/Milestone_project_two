The Granite Trail

3 hours 2.8 miles (4.5km) Moderate
  1. Nearest Town/ Village
    Newcastle
  2. Starting point
    Granite Trail Newcastle (sign posted)
  3. Ending Point
    Donard Car park (then a walk through Newcastle to the beginning of the trail)
  4. Terrain
    Steep incline, rough trail, steps, forest track, Stiles. Circular Trail.
  5. Facilities
    A full range of facilities are available in Newcastle including: Parking Toilet
