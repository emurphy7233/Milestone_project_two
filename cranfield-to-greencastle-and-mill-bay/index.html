Cranfield to Greencastle and Mill Bay

3 hours 5 miles (8 km) Easy
  1. Nearest Town/ Village
    Kilkeel
  2. Starting point
    Cranfield Caravan park
  3. Ending Point
    The same as starting point
  4. Terrain
    Coastal- beach is rough and rocky in places so sturdy footwear is recommended. Trail is not passable at high tide. Linear trail.
  5. Facilities
    Parking Toilet Cafe Caravan park
