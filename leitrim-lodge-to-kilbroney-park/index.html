Leitrim Lodge to Kilbroney Park

3 hours 30 mins 12.5 miles (20.1 km) Strenuous
  1. Nearest Town/ Village
    Rostrevor and Hilltown
  2. Starting point
    Leitrim Lodge Car Park
  3. Ending Point
    Kilbroney Park
  4. Terrain
    Boots required. Completely off-road, steep gradients, Linear trail.
  5. Facilities
    Parking (at both ends) Toilet (in Kilbroney)
